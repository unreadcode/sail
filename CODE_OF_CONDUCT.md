# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

**English** | [中文](#中文)

Based on the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) v2.1.

## Our pledge

To foster an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Standards

**Encouraged:**

- Being kind and respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback; taking responsibility and apologizing
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable:**

- Harassment, personal attacks, insulting or derogatory comments
- Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct clearly inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report violations privately to **i@unreadcode.com**. All reports will be reviewed seriously and kept confidential.

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, etc. that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently ban offenders, explaining the reason where appropriate.

# 中文

[English](#code-of-conduct--行为准则) | **中文**

本项目基于 [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org)（贡献者公约）v2.1 精神制定。

## 我们的承诺

为营造开放、友善的社区环境，我们承诺：无论年龄、体型、身体或精神状况、国籍、种族、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济地位、宗教信仰或性取向如何，参与本项目都应是无骚扰的体验。

## 行为标准

**鼓励：**

- 友善、尊重不同观点与经验
- 接受建设性批评，主动承担并致歉
- 以社区整体利益为重

**不可接受：**

- 骚扰、人身攻击、侮辱性或贬损性言论
- 涉性的言语或图像、不受欢迎的性关注
- 公开或私下骚扰
- 未经许可公开他人隐私信息
- 其他在专业场合明显不当的行为

## 执行

如遇违反行为准则的情况，请通过 **i@unreadcode.com** 私下举报。所有举报都会被认真、保密地处理。

维护者有权对违反者删除评论、提交、issue 等内容，或暂时 / 永久禁止其参与，并在适当时说明理由。
